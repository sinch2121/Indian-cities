Fun Facts About Mumbai

🏙
Mumbai is home to over 20 million people!
This makes it one of the most populous cities in the world, often referred to as the financial capital of India!
🎬
The city is the heart of Bollywood, producing the most films annually worldwide.
Mumbai is home to over 1,000 films produced each year, captivating audiences globally!
🚂
The Mumbai Suburban Railway, operating since 1853, is one of the busiest railways in the world!
Transporting over 7.5 million passengers daily, it's a lifeline for Mumbaikars!
🌊
The iconic Marine Drive is also called the "Queen's Necklace" because of the streetlights that form a necklace shape at night.
It's a popular spot for locals and tourists to enjoy breathtaking sunsets!
🏛️
The Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus is a UNESCO World Heritage Site!
This historic railway station is an architectural marvel and a vital transport hub in the city!
🕌
Mumbai is home to the largest number of art deco buildings outside of Miami!
These stunning structures showcase a unique architectural style and reflect the city's rich cultural heritage!
